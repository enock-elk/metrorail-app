    User Guide & Tips

    üß≠ The Trip Planner

    Our planner automatically finds the fastest connection between two points. It currently supports:

    • Direct Trips: Trains that go straight from A to B.
    • Single Transfers: Trips involving ONE change at a major hub.
    • Bridge Trips: Complex journeys using TWO transfers (up to 3 trains) to bridge across different corridors.

    Important Note:

    Transfers within the same route/corridor are treated as a single continuous journey.

    üó∫Ô∏è Using the Map

    Unsure of the path? Check the Network Map in the menu. Visually trace your route to see if you need to switch lines (colors).
