    User Guide & Tips

    üß≠ The Trip Planner

    Our planner is designed to find the fastest connection between two points. It currently supports:

    • Direct Trips: Trains that go straight from A to B.
    • Single Transfers: Trips involving ONE change at a major hub (e.g., Pretoria or Germiston) to switch lines.

    Important Note:

    Transfers within the same route/corridor (e.g. getting off at Hercules to wait for another train on the same line) are treated as a single continuous journey. They do not count as a "Transfer" in the planner results.

    üó∫Ô∏è Using the Map

    Unsure of the path? Check the Network Map in the menu. Visually trace your route to see if you need to switch lines (colors).

    *Tip: If your trip requires multiple transfers, try planning it in legs (e.g. "From Home to Pretoria", then "From Pretoria to Work").
