    Help & Tips

    1 Trip Planner Limits

    The Trip Planner currently supports Direct Trips and Single Transfers (max 2 trains).
    Complex journeys requiring 3 or more trains (multi-hop) are not yet supported.

    2 Offline Mode

    Schedules are automatically saved to your device. You can open the app and check times even without data or Wi-Fi.

    3 Smart Updates

    The app checks for schedule changes in the background so you don't have to. Check the "Last Updated" date on the home screen to be sure.

    Note: All times are estimates based on official Metrorail schedules. Delays or cancellations may occur that are not reflected here immediately.
