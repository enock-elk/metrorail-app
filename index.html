User Guide & Tips

🧭 Trip Planner

Our new Dijkstra routing engine maps out unlimited available connections between your origin and destination, calculating the absolute fastest paths across the entire Metrorail network.

  • Direct Trips: Uninterrupted service to your destination.
  • Multi-Transfers: Dynamic routing bridging multiple lines automatically.

Sorting Priority

Results are prioritized by departure time. We encourage you to review the later options on the list - a direct train departing slightly later is often faster than taking an immediate multi-transfer route.

Smart Connections

The engine is optimized for Metrorail's physical infrastructure. It includes 0-minute platform relays, as trains are often held at hubs (like Koedoespoort or Germiston) for immediate cross-platform connections.

🗺️ Using the Map

Unsure of the path? Check the Network Map in the menu. Visually trace your route to see if you need to switch lines (colors).

⚡ Offline Capable

Lost signal? No problem. The app automatically saves schedules to your device, so you can check times even in tunnels or dead zones.

Unofficial & Independent

🙋🏾‍♂️ The idea for Next Train first sparked in 2023, but it was during mid-November 2025, that I finally began building the WebApp with a simple mission: to replace blurry notice photos and crowded station boards with clear, digital accuracy.

What began as a tool for my own family has rapidly grown to help thousands of commuters, racking up over 120,000 page views in just a few months. While this remains an independent passion project (not an official PRASA tool), it is built with the reliability and care our growing community deserves.
